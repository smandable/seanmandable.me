Debt Descent is a native debt-payoff planner for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Add your debts, compare the Snowball and Avalanche methods, and see exactly when you'll be debt-free — all on your device, with no account and nothing uploaded.

### Frequently asked questions

**Is my financial data private?**
Yes — completely. Everything stays on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and no analytics. See the [Privacy Policy](/debt-descent/privacy).

**What does Debt Descent Pro include, and how much is it?**
Pro is a **one-time $9.99 purchase** (no subscription) that unlocks all seven payoff methods, the what-if slider and per-debt payoff tests, snowflakes, refinance modeling, amortization schedules, PDF/calendar/JSON export, and due-date reminders. One purchase unlocks Pro on your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

**I bought Pro but don't see it on a new device.**
It should unlock automatically when you're signed in with the same Apple ID. If not, open the paywall (tap any "PRO" feature, or the ••• menu → "Unlock Debt Descent Pro") and tap **Restore Purchases**.

**How do I import a statement?**
Use **Import Statement** (the ••• menu, or File ▸ Import Statement on Mac) and pick a PDF. Debt Descent reads the balance, APR, minimum, and due date on your device.

**How do I back up my data?**
Pro users can **Export to JSON** (••• menu / File menu) to save a complete backup, and **Import from JSON** to restore it on any device.

**How do I start over?**
The ••• menu has **Load Sample Data** (a fabricated demo set) and **Purge All Data** (erases everything and starts blank).
