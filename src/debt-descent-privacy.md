_Last updated: June 28, 2026_

**Debt Descent does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any of your data. Full stop.** There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no analytics or tracking.

### What stays on your device

Everything you enter — your debts, balances, APRs, payments, planned windfalls, and settings — is stored **only on your device** (and, if you use iCloud device backup, inside your own encrypted Apple backup). I never see it and it never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

### Statement import

If you import a statement, the PDF is read **entirely on your device** using Apple frameworks (PDFKit, Vision, and Apple Intelligence). The document and its contents are never uploaded anywhere.

### Exports you initiate

PDF, calendar (.ics), and JSON exports are created on your device and saved wherever **you** choose. I am not involved in and have no access to what you do with an exported file.

### Notifications

If you turn on due-date reminders, they are scheduled as **local notifications** on your device. No reminder data is sent anywhere.

### Purchases

The optional one-time "Debt Descent Pro" upgrade is handled entirely by **Apple** via the App Store. Apple processes the payment; I never receive or store your payment information. The app only receives Apple's signed confirmation that the purchase exists, so it can unlock Pro features.

### Third parties

Debt Descent includes **no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs**.

### Children

Debt Descent is rated 4+ and collects no data from anyone, including children.

### Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
